Keep track of your passwords.


Organize and securely store passwords and PIN codes! Passwordcog frees you the headache caused by lost passwords, forgotten PINs, access codes, logins, usernames, and any other sensitive information. Keep your confidential data safe in one place!


Features:

  • Saves passwords in one of the 10 available categories.
  • Sorts accounts by alphabetical order, or order them manually.
  • Requires a master passcode to access the app.
  • Auto-locks to protect the app from prying eyes.
  • Optionally erases all data in case of many failed unlock attempts.
  • Synchronizes with iCloud.
